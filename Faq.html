Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What are your office hours?

Our office hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. However, we provide emergency dental services 24/7 for urgent dental issues.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling us at +91 7908879834, emailing us at info@dentalhospital.com, or using our online booking form on the homepage. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Please contact our office with your insurance information, and our staff will help verify your coverage and benefits. We also offer flexible payment options for those without insurance.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card (if applicable), a list of current medications, and any previous dental records or X-rays. If you're experiencing pain or discomfort, please inform us when scheduling your appointment.

Treatment & Services

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for routine checkups and cleanings. However, some patients may need more frequent visits based on their oral health condition. Our dentists will provide personalized recommendations during your visit.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Yes, professional teeth whitening performed by our dental team is safe and effective. We use clinically proven whitening agents and techniques that minimize sensitivity while achieving excellent results. Results typically last 1-3 years depending on lifestyle factors.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last 20-30 years or even a lifetime. Success depends on factors such as oral hygiene, regular dental visits, overall health, and lifestyle habits like smoking.

Are root canals painful?

Modern root canal therapy is typically no more uncomfortable than getting a large filling. We use advanced techniques and anesthesia to ensure your comfort throughout the procedure. Most patients experience minimal discomfort during and after treatment.

How long does orthodontic treatment take?

Treatment time varies depending on the complexity of your case. Traditional braces typically take 18-36 months, while clear aligners may take 12-24 months. Dr. Smith will provide a personalized treatment timeline during your consultation.

Emergency Care

What constitutes a dental emergency?

Dental emergencies include severe tooth pain, knocked-out teeth, broken or cracked teeth, lost fillings or crowns, abscesses, and injuries to the mouth or jaw. If you're experiencing severe pain or trauma, contact us immediately.

What should I do if I knock out a tooth?

Handle the tooth by the crown (not the root), rinse gently with water if dirty, and try to reinsert it into the socket. If that's not possible, keep it moist in milk or saliva. Contact us immediately - time is critical for successful reimplantation.

How quickly can you see me for an emergency?

We prioritize emergency cases and typically can see emergency patients within a few hours during business hours. For after-hours emergencies, call our emergency line and we'll provide immediate guidance and arrange urgent care if needed.

Payment & Insurance

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and bank transfers. We also offer financing options and payment plans to help make dental care more affordable for our patients.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for extensive treatments. Our staff will work with you to create a payment schedule that fits your budget. We believe that financial constraints shouldn't prevent you from receiving quality dental care.

Can I get a cost estimate before treatment?

Absolutely! We provide detailed treatment plans with cost estimates for all procedures. This allows you to make informed decisions about your dental care and plan your budget accordingly. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Hygiene & Safety

What safety measures do you have in place?

We follow strict sterilization protocols, use disposable items when possible, and maintain the highest standards of infection control. Our equipment is regularly serviced and our staff receives ongoing training in safety procedures.

How do you sterilize your instruments?

All instruments are thoroughly cleaned and sterilized using hospital-grade autoclaves. We use biological indicators to verify sterilization effectiveness and maintain detailed records of all sterilization cycles for quality assurance.
