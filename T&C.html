BrightSmile Dental Hospital provides comprehensive dental care services including but not limited to:

All services are provided by licensed dental professionals in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Appointment Policy

Scheduling

Appointments can be scheduled by phone, email, or through our online booking system. We recommend scheduling routine appointments at least two weeks in advance.

Cancellation Policy

We require at least 24 hours notice for appointment cancellations or rescheduling. Failure to provide adequate notice may result in a cancellation fee equivalent to 50% of the scheduled treatment cost.

No-Show Policy

Patients who fail to show up for scheduled appointments without prior notice will be charged a no-show fee. Repeated no-shows may result in termination of the patient-doctor relationship.

Late Arrival

Patients arriving more than 15 minutes late may need to reschedule their appointment, depending on the schedule and treatment requirements.

Payment Plans

Payment plans may be available for extensive treatments. All payment plan agreements must be established before treatment begins and signed by the patient.

Outstanding Balances

Accounts with outstanding balances may be subject to interest charges and collection procedures. Patients with outstanding balances may not receive future non-emergency treatment until the account is current.
